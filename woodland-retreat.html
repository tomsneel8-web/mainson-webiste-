About Us

Welcome to
Woodland Retreat

Hosted by Emily and nestled deep in the tranquil forests of Evergreen Valley, Woodland Retreat offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern comfort. Our mission is to provide a peaceful escape where you can relax, recharge, and immerse yourself in the beauty of nature. Built in 2019, our cozy cabin combines warm, inviting interiors with breathtaking surroundings, making it an ideal getaway for couples, families, and solo travelers alike.

Accommodation
Our Location

Evergreen
Valley

Tucked away in the heart of the woods, Evergreen Valley is a serene haven just a 40-minute drive from Mountain Lodge Airport. The area is a paradise for nature lovers, featuring scenic hiking trails, crystal-clear lakes, and an abundance of wildlife. Spend your days exploring dense pine forests, kayaking on quiet waters, or simply enjoying the peaceful ambiance from your private deck. As the sun sets, cozy up by the fire and gaze at the star-filled sky.

What We Offer

Everything You Need
to Disconnect & Restore

Every detail of Woodland Retreat has been thoughtfully designed to make your stay feel effortless, warm, and deeply restorative.

🔥

Private Fire Pit

Gather under the stars around your own private fire pit. Logs are provided — just bring the marshmallows and the good company.

🌲

Forest Trails Access

Step directly onto miles of secluded hiking and walking trails through old-growth forest, wildflower meadows and riverside paths.

🛁

Outdoor Hot Tub

Soak in the cedar hot tub surrounded by the sounds of the forest. Available year-round — especially magical in winter snow.

📶

Intentional Off-Grid Stay

Wi-Fi is available but deliberately slow. We encourage you to trade the scroll for a good book and a glass of wine on the deck.

🍳

Fully-Equipped Kitchen

A chef-ready kitchen stocked with essentials, local spices, and fresh coffee from the valley's own roastery for your arrival morning.

🌅

Sunrise Deck

The wraparound deck faces east — designed for golden-hour mornings with a coffee in hand and birdsong as your soundtrack.
